About

EquityExplorer provides a platform where information, analysis, evaluation for stock, equity, commodity market and various sectors can be done. Expert analysis and prediction feature allows to review the experts.Website help individuals gain knowledge about various companies, economic news and financials etc. As lot of people started investing in stock market during the pandemic period,many people feel the data available on the stocks and the market is in complex form or paid. Here we can help the people with the restrictions.

The objective of this website is as follows:
1. To study the current data analyzing techniques and identify their limitations that may help to suggest a hybrid approach which may overcomes the drawbacks existing methods.
2. To simplify the data of the companies using graphs and charts.this makes new investors to understand more easily the stock performance and to make their decisions.
3. To give the data free of cost and to recommend the stocks by studying the past performances of the stock.
